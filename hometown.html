Jakarta

Jakarta, the capital city of Indonesia. It's a busy metropolis known for it's rich culture and heritage due to everyone from every place in indonesia has an interest to have a career here, dynamic economy, and it's significant historical landmark. Jakarta is the largest city in Southeast Asia, home to over 10 million people, with a greater metropolitan area (Jabodetabek) housing more than 30 million residents, making it one of the world's largest urban agglomerations. It is a melting pot of ethnicities, including Javanese, Sundanese, Chinese Indonesians, and Betawi, reflecting Indonesia's motto of "Unity in Diversity." The city is known for its iconic landmarks like the National Monument (Monas), the colonial-era Kota Tua, and the Istiqlal Mosque, as well as its modern skyline filled with high-rises, luxury hotels, and vast shopping malls
